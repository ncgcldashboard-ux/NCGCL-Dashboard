📈 How Savings Grow

Fixed CSC is constant. As load factor increases, that fixed charge spreads over more kWh, reducing average cost.

Example: B3 at 45% LF saves PKR 13.1M. At 70% LF, savings jump to PKR 44.5M (+240%).

🎯 Inflection Point

TPT becomes cheaper than current tariff when fixed costs are spread over sufficient consumption.

For most categories, inflection occurs around 40–50% load factor, depending on rate structure.
