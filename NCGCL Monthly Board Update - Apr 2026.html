Investment Portfolio Performance

April 2026 Investment Review · Total portfolio PKR 9.58 Bn · Policy rate 11.50% effective April 27

Portfolio outperformance sustained despite interest rate volatility. Notwithstanding the 100 bps policy rate hike to 11.50% on April 27 and heightened market pressure across the yield curve, NCGCL's investment portfolio delivered a positive MTD alpha of +23 bps against the peer fund universe average of 8.87%, and a YTD alpha of +43 bps against the peer benchmark of 9.31%. This places NCGCL among a select minority of institutions: 37 out of 44 high-rated fixed income and money market funds underperformed their benchmark during April.
YTD performance vs absolute benchmark (3M KIBOR −100 bps). Measured against the absolute benchmark of 3M KIBOR less 100 bps — equivalent to 9.87% YTD — NCGCL's YTD return of 9.74% trailed the benchmark by −14 bps. The variance reflects strategic sovereign allocation and capital gains timing consistent with the investment mandate, and is to be read alongside the +43 bps YTD alpha generated over the peer fund universe.
