⭐ Historic Milestone — First Credit Guarantee Issued · May 11, 2026
NCGCL issued its first-ever credit guarantee on May 11, 2026 — a PKR 250 Mn coverage in respect of payment obligations of Kashf Foundation under the PMIC facility. Portfolio restructuring complete — alpha sustained through volatile rates. The decisive reallocation of all mutual fund holdings into Market Treasury Bills in May 2026 has transformed the portfolio's income profile. NCGCL delivered a monthly return of 10.83%, ranking 9th of 43 rated funds and generating +94 bps alpha above the peer average of 9.89%. On a YTD basis, NCGCL's return of 9.61% outperforms the peer average of 9.34% by +27 bps. In May alone, 41 of 43 high-rated fixed income and money market funds underperformed the benchmark. NCGCL did not — and now exceeds the absolute benchmark (3M KIBOR less 100 bps) on a monthly basis for the first time in 2026, delivering +13 bps above the 11.00% benchmark yield.
