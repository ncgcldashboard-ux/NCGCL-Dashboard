Risk-Sharing Layers ‚Äì Simple Explainer & Product Ladder
Context Slide (Before Transactions)
What the Layers Mean
  • First-loss (FL): NCGCL absorbs the earliest losses up to an agreed % cap; protects the senior exposure most directly.
  • Second-loss (SL): NCGCL pays only after the first-loss layer is fully used (often SBP/other first-loss), then covers the next slice.
  • Pari passu (PP): Losses (and recoveries) are shared pro-rata at the same level from day one, up to the cap.
  • Mezzanine provisioning (MPO): A provisioning/FSV benefit window (accounting relief) that is not treated as contingent guarantee exposure.
How to Read the ‚ÄúRisk Ladder‚Äù
  • Lower rungs generally consume more NCGCL capital per PKR deployed (higher loss absorption priority).
  • Higher rungs preserve stronger PFI ‚Äúskin-in-the-game‚Äù and are usually more scalable per unit capital.
  • In practice, structures are often layered to optimise incentives and capital efficiency.
Transaction-to-Category Map
  • BIPL ‚Äì Agri Input (ST): Second-loss (NCGCL) over SBP first-loss.
  • BIPL ‚Äì LT Agri & Assets: First-loss (pilot at 10%, scalable to 15%).
  • PMIC ‚Äì Microfinance PCG: Pari passu (50% pro-rata).
  • HBL MFB ‚Äì PCG + MPO: Pari passu layer + Mezzanine provisioning (MPO).
  • UNIDO ‚Äì Sindh Consortium: Blended portfolio cover (single-layer portfolio guarantee with pilot cap).

BIPL ‚Äì Smallholder Agri Input Finance (6-Month Cycle)
Action: Approve 15% Second-Loss
Product Type
Seasonal smallholder agri input portfolio (Islamic structure)
Guarantee Cover
10% NCGCL second-loss (above 10% SBP first-loss)
NCGCL Exposure
PKR 100m cap on PKR 1.0bn portfolio
Facility Size
PKR 1.0bn
  • Revolving finance for inputs (fertiliser, seed, pesticides etc.).
  • Self-liquidating at harvest; 6-month bullet loans.
Guarantee Structure
  • SBP: 10% first-loss on the portfolio.
  • NCGCL: additional 10% second-loss above SBP‚Äôs layer.
  • BIPL: retains remaining 80% of ultimate loss.
Tenor & Pricing
  • Tenor: up to 6 months.
  • Guarantee fee: 1.0% flat, charged at maturity.
  • Pricing aligned with approved Shariah-compliant grid.
Eligibility & Portfolio Criteria
  • Smallholders up to 12.5 acres; KYC and CNIC verified.
  • Internal and ECIB checks with no severe delinquency history.
  • Diversified by crop and geography; caps on single-borrower and group exposure.
Controls, Reporting & Claims
  • Mandatory Takaful; standardised documents and digital MIS.
  • Monthly reporting on disbursements, PAR (Portfolio At Risk) and recoveries.
  • Claims admissible only after SBP‚Äôs 10% first-loss layer is fully exhausted and recoveries documented.
Key Risks & Mitigants
  • Weather / yield risk: short tenor, geographic diversification, insurance.
  • Moral hazard: high residual exposure for BIPL; hard PAR trip-wires and Risk Acceptance Criteria (RAC) compliance.
  • Operational risk: standardised processes, audit rights, independent monitoring.
Decision Sought: Approve PKR 1.0bn smallholder agri input portfolio with NCGCL providing 10% second-loss cover (PKR 100m cap) over SBP‚Äôs 10% first-loss, subject to Risk Acceptance Criteria (RAC) and PAR triggers.

Product Type

PKR 100m cap on ~PKR 660m portfolio
Indicative Portfolio Size
~PKR 660m*
  • Term loans for mechanisation, solar, storage, livestock and other productive assets.
  • *Phased ceiling; scale-up in both size and coverage (up to 15% FL) linked to pilot outcomes.
Guarantee Structure
  • Initial cohort to launch with NCGCL first-loss cover at 10% of the portfolio.
  • Subject to satisfactory performance and Risk Acceptance Criteria (RAC) / Board sign-off, first-loss cover may be increased up to 15% on future cohorts within the same programme.
  • NCGCL exposure for the initial phase capped at PKR 100m across the portfolio; residual loss retained by BIPL.
Tenor & Pricing
  • Tenor: up to 8 years, amortising.
  • Guarantee fee: 1.0% p.a. on guaranteed outstanding (pilot phase).
  • Pricing grid and any adjustment for higher first-loss coverage (up to 15%) to be calibrated based on pilot performance and portfolio risk.
Eligibility & Risk Criteria
  • Borrowers with verifiable cashflows to service term debt.
  • Compliance with BIPL policy and NCGCL Risk Acceptance Criteria (RAC), including sector caps and concentration limits.
  • Hard PAR (Portfolio At Risk) threshold (e.g. 10%) at portfolio level triggers review, pause in utilisation and reconsideration of any move towards 15% coverage.
Security & Safeguards
  • e-Passbook where applicable; charge over financed assets and receivables.
  • Mandatory insurance / Takaful on financed assets; standard valuation norms.
  • Monthly reporting on disbursements, PAR (Portfolio At Risk) and recoveries; right to pause utilisation or coverage escalation if metrics deteriorate.
Key Risks & Mitigants
  • Asset and price risk mitigated by rigorous appraisal, collateralisation and stress testing.
  • Pilot structure at 10% first-loss limits initial capital consumption while crowding in BIPL capital; potential move to 15% contingent on strong performance.
  • Explicit pilot review to inform eventual scale-up, recalibration of coverage and pricing, or redeployment of capacity.
Decision Sought: Approve a long-term agri & productive asset PCG pilot with NCGCL first-loss cover at 10% of the portfolio (PKR 100m cap), with an explicit provision to scale up to 15% first-loss on subsequent cohorts subject to Risk Acceptance Criteria (RAC) thresholds, PAR and overall pilot performance.

HBL Microfinance Bank; PCG + MPO Window
Action: Approve 25% Pari Passu PCG & MPO Facility
Product Type
Tiered portfolio credit guarantee and mezzanine provisioning offset on IFC-backed micro & agri portfolio.
Guarantee Cover
25% account-level pari passu guarantee (incremental lending) + MPO FSV benefit window.
NCGCL Exposure & MPO
PCG cap PKR 1.0bn (contingent capital) + MPO allocation PKR 500m (FSV benefit, non-contingent).
Purpose & Facility Size
PKR 1.0bn PCG + PKR 0.5bn MPO
  • Provide partial risk coverage to HBL MFB for lending to micro borrowers, microenterprises and smallholder farmers.
  • Anchored on existing IFC 50% risk participation to crowd-in additional private capital.
  • Facility structure:
    • PKR 1.0bn ‚Äì 25% pari passu PCG layer on eligible incremental portfolio; and
    • PKR 0.5bn ‚Äì MPO allocation providing FSV-based provisioning relief on eligible NPL portfolio.
Guarantee Structure
  • IFC provides 50% account-level coverage on the portfolio.
  • NCGCL adds a 25% account-level pari passu layer: aggregate cover up to 75% at account level.
  • NCGCL PPG exposure is capped at PKR 1.0bn ‚Äì this is the only component counted as contingent capital.
  • MPO: up to PKR 500m FSV benefit on eligible NPL portfolio ‚Äì treated as a mezzanine provisioning offset, not as contingent capital exposure.
Tenor & Pricing
  • Tenor: up to 3 years, aligned with underlying micro and agri cycles.
  • PPG fee: 1.50% p.a. on guaranteed portion of average outstanding principal, payable quarterly in arrears.
  • MPO fee: 1.00% p.a. on average FSV benefit utilised, payable quarterly in arrears.
Eligibility & Covenants
  • Eligible portfolios: micro, microenterprise and smallholder farmer financings; housing exposures excluded as per mandate.
  • Policy Rule (Infection Ratio Trigger): ‚â§ 8%; breach triggers portfolio freeze and joint review.
  • Minimum Capital Adequacy Ratio: ‚â• 12% throughout facility life.
  • Facility governed by NCGCL Guarantee Policy 2025 and Risk Acceptance Criteria (RAC), including sector and geographic caps.
Monitoring, Reporting & Claims
  • Monthly portfolio data submissions: utilisation, PAR, NPLs, provisions and MPO FSV usage.
  • Quarterly joint review meetings to assess data integrity, covenant compliance, concentration and remedial actions.
  • Claims admissible post verified-loss condition and exhaustion of internal recoveries by HBL MFB and IFC processes.
  • Standardised documentation: loan files, recovery evidence and loss confirmations to accompany each claim.
Key Risks & Mitigants
  • Portfolio credit risk: mitigated through layered loss absorption (IFC 50%, NCGCL 25%) and hard PAR/NPL covenants.
  • Operational / reporting risk: controlled via monthly data feeds, standard templates and joint audit rights.
  • Liquidity & funding risk at HBL MFB: offset by strong sponsor support and comfortable CAR / leverage profile.
  • Regulatory and conversion risk: managed under SBP oversight and HBL group Islamic conversion roadmap.

  • A Mezzanine Provisioning Offset window of up to PKR 500m FSV benefit on eligible NPL portfolio (non-contingent provisioning relief),
