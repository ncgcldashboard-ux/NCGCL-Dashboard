BIPL ‚Äì Smallholder Agri Input Finance (6-Month Cycle)
Action: Approve 10% Second-Loss Pilot
Product Type
Seasonal smallholder agri input portfolio (Islamic structure)
Guarantee Cover
10% NCGCL second-loss (over 10% SBP first-loss)
NCGCL Exposure
PKR 100m cap on PKR 1.0bn portfolio
Facility Size
PKR 1.0bn
Revolving portfolio to finance fertilizer, seed and other inputs for smallholders; self-liquidating at harvest.
Guarantee Structure
  • SBP provides a 10% first-loss layer on the portfolio.
  • NCGCL provides a further 10% second-loss layer, above SBP‚Äôs first-loss.
  • BIPL retains the remaining 80% ultimate loss on the portfolio.
  • Claims on NCGCL are admissible only after SBP‚Äôs layer is fully utilized and losses are validated.
Tenor & Pricing
  • 6-month bullet loans aligned with the crop cycle.
  • Guarantee fee of 1.0% flat, charged at maturity.
  • Pricing consistent with approved grid and the Shariah-compliant structure.
Eligibility & Portfolio Criteria
  • Small farmers with landholding up to 12.5 acres, verified CNIC and KYC completed.
  • Internal and ECIB checks; no severe delinquency history.
  • Portfolio diversification across crop types and regions; caps on single-borrower and group exposure.
Controls, Reporting & Claims
  • Mandatory Takaful; standardized loan documentation and digital MIS.
  • Monthly reporting on disbursements, PAR (Portfolio At Risk) and recoveries; joint quarterly portfolio reviews.
  • NCGCL claims admissible only once SBP 10% first-loss is fully absorbed, PAR thresholds tested, and recovery efforts documented.
Key Risks & Mitigants
  • Weather and yield risk: short tenor, geographic diversification, and insurance.
  • Moral hazard: high residual exposure for BIPL; hard PAR trip-wires and RAC compliance.
  • Operational risk: standardized processes, audit rights and independent monitoring.
Decision Sought: Approve PKR 1.0bn BIPL smallholder agri input portfolio with NCGCL providing 10% second-loss cover (PKR 100m maximum exposure), over SBP‚Äôs 10% first-loss, subject to adherence to agreed RAC, PAR (Portfolio At Risk) triggers and reporting standards.

HBL Microfinance Bank ‚Äì Tiered Portfolio Credit Guarantee
Action: Approve Tiered FLG + Second-Loss + MPO Structure
Product Type
Tiered portfolio credit guarantee for micro borrowers, microenterprises and smallholder farmers (Shariah-ready, digitally enabled).
Guarantee Cover
10% First-Loss Guarantee (FLG) on a PKR 5bn portfolio (portfolio level),
25% account-level second-loss guarantee on a PKR 2bn portfolio alongside IFC, and
25% Mezzanine Provisioning Offset (MPO) on the IFC portfolio (up to PKR 250m FSV benefit).
NCGCL Exposure
Maximum contingent liability of PKR 1.25bn across three tranches:
PKR 500m FLG + PKR 500m second-loss + PKR 250m MPO.
Portfolio Size & Tenor
Utilisation up to PKR 8.0bn ¬∑ Tenor up to 3 Years
  • Facility structured around:
    • PKR 5bn portfolio under 10% first-loss coverage (FLG),
    • PKR 2bn portfolio under IFC + NCGCL account-level coverage, and
    • PKR 1bn portfolio eligible for MPO-linked FSV benefits (up to PKR 250m).
  • Aggregate pilot utilisation assumed at ~PKR 8bn over the guarantee period.
  • Overall guarantee tenor of up to 3 years, aligned with underlying retail portfolios.
Facility Purpose & Repayment Source
  • Purpose: provide partial risk coverage to HBL MFB for lending to:
    • micro borrowers and microenterprises, and
    • smallholder farmers in agri and agri-linked value chains.
  • Repayment source: cashflows from HBL MFB‚Äôs underlying MSME and agri loan portfolios.
  • Borrower repayments service the guaranteed pool, preserving cashflow integrity.
Pricing
  • FLG: Guarantee fee of 2.00% p.a. on the guaranteed portion of average outstanding principal, payable quarterly in arrears.
  • Second-loss (account level): Guarantee fee of 1.50% p.a. on the guaranteed portion of average outstanding principal, payable quarterly in arrears.
  • MPO: Guarantee fee of 1.00% p.a. on the average FSV benefit utilized (up to PKR 250m), payable quarterly in arrears.
Guarantee Structure ‚Äì Layered Risk Sharing
  • First-Loss Guarantee (FLG):
    • 10% first-loss cover at portfolio level on a PKR 5bn pool (maximum NCGCL liability PKR 500m).
    • Linked initially to seasonal livestock / Bakra Eid facility and similar short-term working capital pools.
  • Account-Level Second Loss (aligned with IFC):
    • IFC provides 50% coverage at account level; NCGCL adds a further 25% second-loss layer.
    • Aggregate account-level coverage of up to 75% (50% IFC + 25% NCGCL) on eligible loans.
    • NCGCL‚Äôs second-loss is activated only once IFC has honored claims totalling 25% of the portfolio.
    • Claims then shared pro-rata: IFC 50%, NCGCL 25% on incremental losses thereafter.
  • Mezzanine Provisioning Offset (MPO):
    • No direct credit coverage; instead, NCGCL allows HBL MFB to take FSV benefit on NPLs up to PKR 250m.
    • FSV benefit improves P&L by lowering provisioning charges until accounts reach ‚ÄúLoss‚Äù status and IFC claims are lodged.
  • Residual risk beyond these layers is retained by HBL MFB to preserve ‚Äúskin in the game‚Äù.
Eligibility, Covenants & Monitoring
  • Eligible products:
    • seasonal livestock / Bakra Eid dealer finance, and
    • IFC-aligned MSME and agri products for micro and small enterprises.
  • Overall portfolio infection ratio (gross) to be maintained at or below 8%; breaches trigger a portfolio freeze and joint review.
  • Early-warning triggers: PAR (Portfolio At Risk) > 5% or NPL ratio > 10% activates remedial review and potential suspension of further utilization.
  • Minimum capital-adequacy ratio (CAR) covenant: HBL MFB to maintain CAR ‚â• 12%.
  • Monthly portfolio data submissions (utilisation, PAR / NPL, vintage and segment mix) and quarterly joint portfolio review meetings between NCGCL and HBL MFB.
  • Full adherence to NCGCL claim protocols, data submission standards and AML/CFT requirements for downstream obligors.
Claims, Recoveries & Key Risks
  • Claims & Recoveries
    • Claims triggered only once exposures meet verified-loss conditions and internal recovery procedures are exhausted.
    • Each claim supported by loan files, recovery evidence and portfolio-level schedules.
    • NCGCL settles admissible claims in line with the approved claims protocol; recoveries shared according to the agreed waterfall.
  • Key Risks & Mitigants
    • Portfolio credit risk: mitigated via layered first-loss, second-loss and MPO structure, tight eligibility criteria and IFRS-9 provisioning discipline.
    • Operational & data risk: addressed through monthly performance data, standardized templates, joint audits and clearly defined claim windows.
    • Liquidity & funding risk: supported by HBL sponsorship, strong equity base and sizeable liquid investment book.
    • Regulatory & Shariah risk: design is Shariah-ready with a defined Islamic conversion plan and compliance under SBP oversight.
Decision Sought: Approve the tiered Portfolio Credit Guarantee facility for HBL Microfinance Bank comprising:
  • 10% first-loss cover on a PKR 5bn portfolio (maximum NCGCL exposure PKR 500m);
  • 25% account-level second-loss guarantee on a PKR 2bn IFC-aligned portfolio (maximum NCGCL exposure PKR 500m); and
  • 25% Mezzanine Provisioning Offset facility on the IFC portfolio, allowing FSV benefit up to PKR 250m.
Aggregate NCGCL contingent liability is capped at PKR 1.25bn, with strong covenants on PAR (Portfolio At Risk), CAR, and reporting, consistent with Guarantee Policy 2025 and NCGCL‚Äôs risk-acceptance framework.
