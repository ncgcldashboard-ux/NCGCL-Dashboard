Systemic Constraints

Weak Energy Access

Idle dehydration plants due to grid unreliability. Requires off-grid solar solutions.

Fragmentation

Small 4-5 kanal orchards prevent scale. Solution: Cooperative aggregation models.

Lack of Finance

Micro-processors lack collateral. Solution: Cash-flow based lending backed by guarantees.

Recommended Policy Action

  • Launch "Heritage Horticulture Guarantee"
  • Finance Solar-Hybrid Cooperatives (Women-led)
  • Standardize "Mountain Heritage" Certification
"The point is not to force capital into the system, but to de-risk those willing to invest sweat and season."
