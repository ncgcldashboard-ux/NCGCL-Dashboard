Deferred

Committee Evaluation

The Committee evaluated proposals from KPMG and EY, with KPMG emerging as the preferred choice on account of a more robust continuous risk-assessment methodology and a successfully negotiated fee reduction to PKR 6 million per annum.

Formal recommendation of the internal auditor has been deferred pending clarification of scope, as set out alongside.

Outstanding Scope Clarifications Requested

  • Explicit inclusion of whistleblowing matter management
  • Identification of control deficiencies
  • Quarterly follow-up on prior recommendations
  • Strict reporting independence from management
Action Item for Management

Obtain a revised proposal from KPMG incorporating the scope points above, and circulate it to the Committee for review.
